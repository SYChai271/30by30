How to participate?

1. Look at the videos provided by the experts within our community
2. Try out planting a simple plant using the kit provided by your community centre
3. Upload your plant to the 30 by 30 website
4. Reap the rewards from successfully completing challenges
5. Use the credits earned to buy groceries from our lcoal farms at the E-Mart
